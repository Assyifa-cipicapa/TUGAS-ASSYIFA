About Matcha Muse

Born from the quiet obsession of a girl who swears by her daily green ritual, this brand is a love letter to matcha in all its gentle elegance. As a forever matcha girl, she believes that matcha is more than just a drink—it’s a mood, a lifestyle, a soft pause in a busy world. Every cup is crafted with care, blending high-quality matcha with delicate flavors to create moments that feel comforting, aesthetic, and a little bit magical.

Our brand embraces femininity in its purest form, soft yet strong, simple yet intentional. From the creamy textures to the calming hues of green, each menu is designed to feel like a warm hug on a slow afternoon. Whether you're chasing focus, peace, or just a pretty drink to match your vibe, we’re here to make your matcha moments feel special, every single time.
